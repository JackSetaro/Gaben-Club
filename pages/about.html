Gabe Newell


Gabe Logan Newell, frequently referred to as Gaben, was born November 3, 1962. Gaben is best known as being the owner of the multi-billion dollar organization
Valve. According to the company, "Valve is an entertainment software and technology company founded in 1996. In addition to creating several of the worlds most
award-winning games, Valve is also a developer of leading-edge technologies including the Source game engine and Steam, the premier online gaming platform."
